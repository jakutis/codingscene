Why?

Coders should meet more often so that they improve their skills faster.

What?

Learn and share programming concepts and implementations.

How?

There will be series of sessions. In each session we present you a task or concept, then you implement it your way and share it in this special web app.

When you're finished, others can see how you did it. If you're struggling - you can see how others did it.

By the way, you can always talk to people. Especially during the meal break!
